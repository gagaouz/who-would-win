Privacy Policy

Animal vs Animal · Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Animal vs Animal ("the app") is designed with privacy in mind, especially for young users. We collect as little data as possible.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not create user accounts or require sign-in.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not share data with advertisers or third-party analytics providers.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following preferences are saved on your device using iOS UserDefaults and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Sound, narration, and haptics on/off settings
  • Total battle count (used to unlock the free Fantasy tier at 50 battles)
  • In-app purchase status (ads removed, Fantasy Pack, Premium subscription)

Battle Requests

When you start a battle, the two animal IDs (e.g. "lion", "tiger") are sent to our API at api.animal-vs-animal.com to generate the battle result. No personal information is included in this request. We log request volume for rate-limiting purposes only; logs are purged automatically and are never sold or shared.

Microphone & Speech Recognition

The app can optionally use your device's microphone and Apple's on-device speech recognition so you can speak an animal name instead of typing. Audio is processed entirely on your device by Apple's framework and is not recorded or transmitted to us.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information. Purchase receipts are verified locally using StoreKit 2.

Children's Privacy

Animal vs Animal is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because we collect no personal information from any user, the app is compliant with COPPA.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at support@animal-vs-animal.com or visit our support page.
